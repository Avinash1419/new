About Us | We are glad to introduce ourselves

GLAD is inspired by the passion to add value to existing college education with professional skill-sets desired by business markets, and thereby create world-class professionals with a brand of their own.


The driving force behind taking GLAD to the next level by offering unique courses designed by Reshma Patra comes from her rich educational background coupled with her association with a global network of highly qualified professionals in various fields of Engineering, Management Consulting, Financial Services, Medicine, Research & Development, Music, Acting, Sports and Publishing.


During her professional tenures working with Smart Analyst India Pvt. Ltd., PricewaterhouseCoopers, and Wipro Consulting Services, Reshma has demonstrated excellence in leadership roles while coaching team-members early in her career, conducting workshops and successfully training teams of 20+ members with professional knowledge and skill-set.


Reshma is a capable professional with an eye for detail and is dedicated towards serving the students to provide the quality of education they deserve. She is an approachable person and delivers services with empathy and a genuine love for everything she does.
