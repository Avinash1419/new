About
EAMCET (Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Examination) 2015 is conducted in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana for students who want to pursue their UG studies in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. EAMCET 2015 is being conducted by JNTU Hyderabad on behalf of Andhra Pradesh State Education Board.

The minimum cutoff for various categories are as follows:-
  • 25% of the total maximum for GE and OBC students
  • No cutoff for SC/ST students.

Earlier, EAMCET was organized by Andhra Pradesh (AP combined state). But from this year onwards, Telangana is having its separate exam as TS EAMCET.
Eligibility
There are certain eligibility rules and criteria decided by the organizing board for students who are appearing for EAMCET 2015. All students who are planning to appear for EAMCET 2015 should clear the following criteria:
  • The candidates must have completed their 17 years of age on or before 31st December 2015.
  • The candidates must belong to Andhra Pradesh state.
  • The candidates must be of Indian Nationality, Overseas Citizen of India – OCI (card holder) or person of India Origin (POI).
  • The candidates must have passed Intermediate examination or its equivalent from a recognized board with Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry or Biology or Biotechnology with minimum 45% marks for General or 40% marks for reserved categories to meet the eligibility of EAMCET 2015.
  • The candidates appearing in 10+2 or its equivalent are also eligible to appear in EAMCET 2015. They have to show their passing certificate at the time of admission.
Exam Pattern
The AP EAMCET 2015 examination will be held on 08th May 2015 and TS EAMCET 2015 examination will be held on 14th May 2015 in two shifts i.e. 10:00 am to 1:00 pm and 2:30 pm to 5:30 pm. the exam will comprise of two papers, one for engineering aspirants and other one for agriculture and medical aspirants. Both papers will consist of 160 objective type questions with 3 hours duration. There is no clause for negative marking. Each correct answer will be rewarded with one mark. The examinations will be asked in two languages as English and Telugu. Only at Hyderabad center, students will be allowed to choose Urdu language in EAMCET 2015. In Each question paper there will be 40 questions based on Physics, 40 questions based on Chemistry and 80 questions based on Mathematics.

Counseling Procedure
The EAMCET web Counseling is conducted by JNTU, Hyderabad. The counseling process proceeds in two phases:
  • In first phase, documents of students will be verified
  • 2ndphase is for counseling and seat allotment in which the candidates will fill choices as per their preferences. After choice filling, seats will be allotted to candidates as per their rank and preference order
After seat allotment candidates have to report to the allotted college for admission process. Every step of counseling has been listed below.
Firstly, candidates will have to verify their documents. After verification, counseling process will begin and comprises of the following steps:
  • Choosing options
  • Enter the colleges as per preferences
  • Filling of choices and locking
  • Modifying choices and submitting
  • Allotment of seats
  • Printing the seating allotment letter, reporting to college.
