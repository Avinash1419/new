About
Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test or BITSAT is a university level Entrance exam conducted by BITS Pilani University for admission into various courses offered by BITS Pilani – Pilani, Hyderabad and Goa Campuses.
BITSAT is an online examination and is conducted on various dates on various centres across various cities and towns. The selection into BITS courses is done by performance in BITSAT as well as your 12th Grade Marks.
Eligibility
  • Candidates must have completed their 12th grade from either ICSE/CBSE or State Board.
  • Candidates writing their 12th grade exams in 2015 are also eligible to write the exam.
  • Candidates must have secured a minimum of 75% in aggregate in PCM Subjects with minimum of 60% in each subject.
  • Candidates who have passed their 10+2 in 2013 or earlier are not eligible.
Exam Information
BITSAT is an Online Examination.
BITSAT has 4 parts and you will be given 3 hours to solve the paper.
BITSAT has a total of 150 questions divided into 4 parts.

1st Part is Physics with 40 questions.
2nd Part is Chemistry with 40 questions.
3rd Part is English Proficiency and Logical Reasoning with 25 questions.
4th Part is Mathematics with 45 questions.

+3/-1 marking scheme throughout the paper will be followed.
